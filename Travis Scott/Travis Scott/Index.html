ABOUT:

Scott grew up in Houston and lived primarily with his grandmother, but later moved to Missouri City, where his father owned his own business. He attended the University of Texas at San Antonio, but dropped out of college in his second year to focus on his music career. After graduating from college, Scott moved to New York in an effort to build his music career. After his parents were told that he had dropped out of school, they cut ties with their son.

On May 21, 2013, he released his first mixtape, Owl Pharaoh, followed by a second, Days Before Rodeo, on August 18, 2014. The latter also features Young Thug, Migos's Quavo, Big Sean and Rich Homie Quan.

Rodeo was Scott's first album. For this album, which was released on September 4, 2015, he collaborated with Justin Bieber, Future and The Weeknd, among others. The album includes the songs Antidote, 90210 and 3500. The album was positively reviewed and landed at number 1 on Billboard's Top Rap Albums in America. This was followed by the albums Birds in the Trap Sing McKnight in 2016 and Astroworld in 2018. Scott also released two mixtapes: one in 2017 in collaboration with Quavo, “Huncho Jack, Jack Huncho”, and one in 2019, “JACKBOYS” together. with the members of the Cactus Jack label. Scott announced in 2020 that he would release his third album called Utopia in 2021. That date was adjusted in May 2022 to before the end of 2022.
